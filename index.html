Welcome

This isn’t just another realtor site. This is a movement. I use real estate to fund healing, hope, and hyperlocal impact. Through every transaction, we give back, tell stories, and restore what was lost.

Vision & Philosophy

My name is Eddie. I’m a community-first realtor, storyteller, and what some call a Relatable Fool — someone who chooses compassion over clout. I built this because I got tired of waiting for someone else to come back for us.

I believe real estate can be a tool for restoration, not just revenue. My movement is rooted in showing up where systems fail — feeding families, showing up to funerals, housing the unheard, and building bridges where there were only cracks.

What is Guided by Eddie™?

Guided by Eddie™ is the public-facing heartbeat of everything I do — real estate with soul. I don’t wear suits to impress you. I show up to serve, to stand with you, and to walk you home.

What is the Ripple Project™?

The Ripple Project is my giveback system — every deal funds acts of service. That includes: groceries, back-to-school drives, wheelchairs for elders, funeral support, and more. It’s how we turn transactions into trust.

What is Raised in CHI™?

Raised in CHI™ is the storytelling series. It’s where I amplify voices from the block to the barbershop. We talk about struggle, legacy, and what it means to be raised in a city that tries to chew you up and forget your name. I make sure that doesn’t happen.

Who is The Relatable Fool™?

That’s me too. It’s my shadow side. It’s where I journal publicly, mess up publicly, and remind you that you don’t need to be perfect to be powerful. The Fool reminds us that presence is worth more than polish.
