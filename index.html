The Ripple Project

Every home I help close fuels community care. 10% of my commission, and often more, is reinvested into what you care about: micro grants, local food programs, ramps for elders, and dignity for the forgotten.

How it funds
Each closing routes a portion to community needs chosen alongside clients.
What it supports
Groceries, school supplies, crisis response, elderly care.
Every closing feeds someone, heals someone, and loves someone who’s forgotten.

It is not charity, it is restoration.
