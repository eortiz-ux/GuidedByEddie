The Ripple Project

Every transaction plants a seed. Ten percent of my commission — and hopefully more — goes directly toward community impact: food, housing, healing. Clients are invited to guide where that money goes, based on what matters to them.

I'd rather be the realtor known for feeding 2,000 people than just selling 200 homes. That’s what Ripple means.

Raised in CHI

This is a storytelling project. We interview real people — neighbors, elders, barbers, immigrants, artists — who make Chicago what it is. We ask deeper questions: Why are you here? What shaped your path? What truth are you living?

It’s not for likes. It’s to build bridges — and help each other.

Guided by Eddie & The Relatable Fool

Guided by Eddie is my daily presence. When you buy, rent, or sell, I want you to feel seen — not sold. You will be educated, not manipulated. This is compassion over commission.

The Relatable Fool is my shadow voice — the raw truths, the questions, the moments I share that others won’t. I speak the things many feel but won’t say. It’s how we question reality, philosophy, and find truth together.

Along the way, I want people to know they are not alone. Through my actions, ideas, and presence — I’ll remind you that you matter.
