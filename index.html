Ripple Project™

Every home I help close fuels community care. 10% of my commission, and often more, is reinvested into what you care about.

How it funds
Each closing routes a portion to needs chosen with clients.
What it supports
Groceries, school supplies, crisis response, ramps for elders.
Foundation
Ripple Impact Foundation™, nonprofit status filed and awaiting approval.

Every closing feeds someone, heals someone, and loves someone who is forgotten. It is not charity, it is restoration.
